## How to access library copies

- **O'Reilly Learning** (formerly Safari) — covers most of the canon, all of testing, all of engineering culture.
- **ACM Digital Library** — covers Code Complete, Pragmatic Programmer, GoF, etc.
- **Local public library** — many of these are popular enough to sit on shelves.
