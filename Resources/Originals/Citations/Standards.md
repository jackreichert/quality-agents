## Not mirrored (copyright restrictions)

| Standard | Holder / License | URL |
|----------|------------------|-----|
| NASA's Power of 10 Rules | © Holzmann / NASA-JPL; freely reproduced widely but originally in IEEE Computer (Jun 2006) | <https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~imarkov/10rules.pdf> (commonly hosted course PDF) |

The Power of 10 paper is short and the *rules themselves* are stated in many derivatives (NASA tech reports, JPL coding standards). Treat the IEEE Computer publication as the canonical citation; most online PDFs are course copies, which are gray-area redistribution.

## Versioning notes

- **OWASP Top 10**: 2021 is current; 2017 still cited in some tooling. Refresh ~every 4 years.
- **OWASP ASVS**: v4.0.3 mirrored. v5 was in late draft as of 2025; check the GitHub repo for current.
- **PEP 8**: living document — refresh occasionally.
- **Airbnb JS Style Guide**: living, but slow-moving since the original maintainers transitioned the repo.
- **Google Style Guides**: per-language, updated independently.
